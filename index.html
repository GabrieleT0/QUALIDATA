ğŸ“– Introduction

This tutorial offers a comprehensive introduction to data quality in the Semantic Web, combining theory and hands-on practice. It covers quality dimensions, FAIR principles, and their application to Linked Data. Participants engage in interactive activities, explore the KGHeartBeat tool through practical tasks, and reflect on broader quality issues such as bias and its practical implications. It targets researchers, practitioners, and developers seeking both foundational understanding and applied skills in quality-aware Linked Data practices.
